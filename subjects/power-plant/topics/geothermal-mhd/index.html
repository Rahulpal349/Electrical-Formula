🌋 Geothermal Resources & Earth Structure
"Geothermal energy is the thermal energy generated and stored in the Earth. It originates from radioactive decay and primordial heat from planet formation. Geothermal gradient: 25–30°C/km."

Temperature Gradient Formula:

$$ T_d = T_{surface} + G_{geo} \times d \quad (G \approx 25\text{-}30\,°C/km) $$
