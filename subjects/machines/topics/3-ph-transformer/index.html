MODULE 4 — 3-Phase Transformer

4.1 Winding Connections & Vector Groups

Star-Star (Yy0 - 0° Shift)

$$ V_{line} = \sqrt{3} V_{phase} $$
$$ I_{line} = I_{phase} $$

Delta-Delta (Dd0 - 0° Shift)

$$ V_{line} = V_{phase} $$
$$ I_{line} = \sqrt{3} I_{phase} $$

Star-Delta (Yd1 - -30° Shift)

LV lags HV by 30°

$$ \frac{V_1}{V_2} = \frac{\sqrt{3} N_1}{N_2} $$

Delta-Star (Dy11 - +30° Shift)

LV leads HV by 30°

$$ \frac{V_1}{V_2} = \frac{N_1}{\sqrt{3} N_2} $$

4.2 Parallel Operation

Load Sharing

$$ I_A = \frac{Z_B}{Z_A + Z_B} \times I_{total} $$
$$ I_B = \frac{Z_A}{Z_A + Z_B} \times I_{total} $$

4.3 Scott Connection

$$ N_{main} = N, \quad V_{main} = V $$
$$ N_{teaser} = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}N = 0.866N $$
$$ V_{teaser} = 0.866 V_{main} $$
Converts 3-phase to 2-phase with 90° phase difference.
