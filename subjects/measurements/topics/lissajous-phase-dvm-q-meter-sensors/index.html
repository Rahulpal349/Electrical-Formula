11.1

Lissajous Patterns: Phase Measurement

Pattern Shape vs Phase Angle

φ = 0° or 360°: Straight line (Q I & III)

0° < φ < 90° or 270° < φ < 360°: Ellipse (Q I & III)

φ = 90° or 270°: Circle

90° < φ < 180° or 180° < φ < 270°: Ellipse (Q II & IV)

φ = 180°: Straight line (Q II & IV)

0°→180°: Clockwise | 180°→360°: Anti-clockwise

Phase Measurement:

Second possibility: φ₂ = 360° − φ
