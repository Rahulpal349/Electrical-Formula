9.4

Energy Meter: Compensations & Errors

Five Compensations:

1. Lag Compensation — Shading coil → θ = 90°+φ

2. Low Load (Friction) — Shading loop → extra torque at light load

3. Anti-Creep — Holes/slots in disc → disc cannot rotate at no load

4. Overload — Saturable shunt on series magnet → linearize

5. Over Voltage — Saturable shunt magnet → limit φsh
