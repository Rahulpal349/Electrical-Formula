📉 5.2 Loss of Charge Method

Exponential Decay · τ = RC · Capacitor Discharge

Discharge Equation

$$ \boxed{V_c(t) = Ve^{-t/\tau}} \quad \tau = RC $$
$$ \boxed{R = \frac{t}{C\ln\!\left(\dfrac{V}{V_c}\right)}} $$

Using log₁₀

$$ \boxed{R = \frac{0.4343\,t}{C\,\log_{10}\!\left(\dfrac{V}{V_c}\right)}} $$

Leakage Correction

$$ \frac{1}{R_{meas}} = \frac{1}{R_x} + \frac{1}{R_C} $$
$$ R_x = \frac{R_{meas} \cdot R_C}{R_C - R_{meas}} $$

Error Sources

Leakage of capacitor itself
Meter loading during measurement
Temperature effects on C

🔧 5.3 Megger (Insulation Resistance Tester)

Electrodynamometer · θ ∝ 1/Rx · Guard Terminal

Deflection Principle

$$ \theta \propto \frac{1}{R_x} $$
$$ \theta = f\!\left(\frac{V}{R_x}\right) $$
No spring — no restoring torque

At Extremes

$$ R_x \to \infty: \; I_{CC} \to 0,\; \theta \to 0\;\;(\infty\text{ mark}) $$
$$ R_x = 0: \; I_{CC} \to \text{max},\; \theta \to \theta_{max}\;\;(0\text{ mark}) $$

Test Voltages

500V — General wiring
1000V — Motors, transformers
2500V, 5000V — Cables, switchgear

Insulation Standards

R ≥ 1 MΩ/kV (general rule)
>100 MΩ = Excellent
1–10 MΩ = Warning
<1 MΩ = FAIL

🌉 5.5 AC Bridges: General Theory

Z₁Z₃ = Z₂Z₄ · Magnitude · Angle · Detectors

Balance Condition

$$ \boxed{\bar{Z}_1 \bar{Z}_3 = \bar{Z}_2 \bar{Z}_4} $$
$$ \text{Magnitude: } |Z_1||Z_3| = |Z_2||Z_4| $$
$$ \text{Angle: } \theta_1 + \theta_3 = \theta_2 + \theta_4 $$

Phase Angles

$R$ only → $\theta = 0°$
$L$ only → $\theta = +90°$
$C$ only → $\theta = -90°$
$R,L$ series → $0° < \theta < 90°$
$R,C$ series → $-90° < \theta < 0°$

Detectors

Vibrant Galvanometer: 5 Hz – 1 kHz
Head Phone: 250 Hz – 4 kHz
Tuned Amplifier: 10 Hz – 100 kHz
Most sensitive at null: HEAD PHONE

📐 5.6 Bridge Balance: Worked Examples

Angle Condition · Maxwell · Hay · Schering

Maxwell L-C

$$ \theta_1 = +\phi_x \;(RL) $$
$$ \theta_4 = -\phi_4 \;(RC\!\parallel) $$
$$ \phi_x = \phi_4 \;\checkmark $$

Hay's Bridge

$$ \theta_1 = +\phi_x \;(RL) $$
$$ \theta_4 = -\phi_4 \;(RC\text{ series}) $$
$$ \phi_x = \phi_4 \;\checkmark $$

Schering

$$ \theta_1 = -\phi_1 \;(RC\text{ series}) $$
$$ \theta_2 = -90° \;(C) $$
$$ \phi_4 = 90° - \phi_1 \;\checkmark $$

Impedance Reference

$Z=R:\;\theta=0°$
$Z=j\omega L:\;\theta=+90°$
$Z=\frac{1}{j\omega C}:\;\theta=-90°$
$Z=R+j\omega L:\;\theta=\arctan\frac{\omega L}{R}$
$Z=R-\frac{j}{\omega C}:\;\theta=-\arctan\frac{1}{\omega CR}$
