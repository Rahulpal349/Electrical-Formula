2.10 String Efficiency

$$ K = \frac{C_{self}}{C_{mutual}} $$
$$ V_1 = \frac{V}{3 + 4K + K^2} $$
$$ \eta = \frac{V_{total}}{n \times V_n} \times 100\% $$
η = 86.0%

2.11 Corona & Critical Voltage

Critical Disruptive Voltage

$$ V_d = m_0 \delta r \cdot E_0 \ln\left(\frac{d}{r}\right) $$

Peek's Corona Loss Formula

$$ P_c = \frac{241}{\delta}(f + 25)\sqrt{\frac{r}{d}}(V - V_d)^2 \times 10^{-5} $$
V < Vd: No Corona Loss

2.12 Sag Calculation & Clearances

Basic Sag

$$ S = \frac{w \cdot l^2}{8T} $$

With Wind & Ice

$$ S = \frac{w_{total} \cdot l^2}{8T} \quad (w_{total} = \sqrt{(w+w_{ice})^2 + w_w^2}) $$
Sag = 2.50 m
