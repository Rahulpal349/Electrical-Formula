2.4 Component Formulas

Boiler Efficiency

$$ \eta_{boiler} = \frac{m_s (h_s - h_{fw})}{m_f \times C_v} \times 100\% $$
$m_s$: steam gen, $h$: enthalpy, $m_f$: fuel, $C_v$: cal. value

Factor of Evaporation

$$ F_e = \frac{h_s - h_{fw}}{2257} $$

Economizer Temp Efficiency

$$ \eta_{eco} = \frac{T_{fw2} - T_{fw1}}{T_{g1} - T_{fw1}} \times 100\% $$

Steam Turbine Power

$$ P_{turbine} = m_s (h_1 - h_2) \text{ kW} $$

T-s Diagram (Rankine Cycle)

Process 1-2: Pump, 2-3: Boiler, 3-4: Turbine, 4-1: Condenser

2.7 Efficiencies & Cooling Tower

Plant Performance

$$ \eta_{thermal} = \frac{P_{output} \times 3600}{m_f \times C_v} \times 100\% $$
$$ HR = \frac{3600}{\eta_{thermal}} \text{ kJ/kWh} $$
$$ SR = \frac{3600}{h_1 - h_2} \text{ kg/kWh} $$

Cooling Tower

$$ \text{Range} = T_{in} - T_{out} $$
$$ \text{Approach} = T_{out} - T_{WBT} $$
$$ \eta_{CT} = \frac{T_{in} - T_{out}}{T_{in} - T_{WBT}} \times 100\% $$

3.2 Nuclear Fission & Energy

Mass-Energy Equivalence

$$ E = mc^2 $$
$$ \Delta m = m_{reactants} - m_{products} $$

Energy from U-235

$$ {}^{235}_{92}U + {}^{1}_{0}n \rightarrow {}^{141}_{56}Ba + {}^{92}_{36}Kr + 3{}^{1}_{0}n + Q $$
$$ Q \approx 200 \text{ MeV} = 3.2 \times 10^{-11} \text{ J} $$

Chain Reaction & Four Factor

$$ k = \frac{\text{Neutrons in gen. } n+1}{\text{Neutrons in gen. } n} $$
$$ k_\infty = \eta \cdot \varepsilon \cdot p \cdot f $$
1 kg U-235 = 2700 tonnes Coal
