AC Circuit Analysis

Unit 2: Mastering alternating current parameters, waveforms, phasors, and resonance with interactive visualizations.

1. Average & RMS Values

Fundamental

$$ V_{avg} = \frac{1}{T}\int_0^T v(t)\,dt \quad ; \quad V_{rms} = \sqrt{\frac{1}{T}\int_0^T v^2(t)\,dt} $$

$$ \text{Peak Factor} = \frac{V_{peak}}{V_{rms}} \quad ; \quad \text{Form Factor} = \frac{V_{rms}}{V_{avg}} $$

2. Signal Waveform Reference

8 Waveforms
$$ V_{rms} = \frac{V_m}{\sqrt{2}} \quad V_{avg} = 0 \quad (\text{Full Cycle}) $$

3. Phasor & Power Fundamentals

Vector Domain

$$ \mathbf{V} = V_{rms}\angle\phi \quad ; \quad Z = R + jX $$

$$ P = VI\cos\phi \quad ; \quad Q = VI\sin\phi \quad ; \quad S = VI $$

4. Series RL Circuit

Lagging

$$ V = \sqrt{V_R^2 + V_L^2} \quad ; \quad \phi = \tan^{-1}(X_L/R) $$

Current LAGS voltage by $\phi$. $\cos\phi$ is lagging.

5. Series RC Circuit

Leading

$$ V = \sqrt{V_R^2 + V_C^2} \quad ; \quad \phi = \tan^{-1}(X_C/R) $$

Current LEADS voltage by $\phi$. $\cos\phi$ is leading.

6. Series RLC & Resonance

Resonance

$$ Z = \sqrt{R^2 + (X_L - X_C)^2} \quad ; \quad f_0 = \frac{1}{2\pi\sqrt{LC}} $$

RESONANCE

7. Parallel RL Circuit

Admittance

$$ I = \sqrt{I_R^2 + I_L^2} \quad ; \quad Y = \sqrt{G^2 + B_L^2} $$

Voltage is common. Current LAGS by $\phi$.

8. Parallel RC Circuit

Admittance

$$ I = \sqrt{I_R^2 + I_C^2} \quad ; \quad Y = \sqrt{G^2 + B_C^2} $$

Voltage is common. Current LEADS by $\phi$.

9. Parallel RLC & Anti-Resonance

Current Control

$$ I = \sqrt{I_R^2 + (I_C - I_L)^2} \quad ; \quad f_{ar} = \frac{1}{2\pi\sqrt{LC}} $$

At resonance, $Z_{max}$ (Dynamic Resistance $L/CR$).
